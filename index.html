Hey, I'm Spencer.

Born in Salt Lake City to an orchestra of siblings - my first language was music and improvising ideas.

Up until 2013 I was performing and running a music studio full time. I loved it, but felt an incompleteness in my creativity as a result of neglecting visual design. I decided to apply to architecture schools and that I'd learn to code.

In 2014 I moved to North Carolina and picked up an internship at McKinney. Which led to a job for a company called 'The Experiment' (aptly named to mark the official beginning of my design career) which led to a job for a start-up out of SF called 'The Grid' which led me to here.

Music is Design

Learning how to play the silence with your instrument is learning how to play the whitespace in a page layout.

Whether I'm playing the guitar or Photoshop or designing an exhibit I'm enthralled with the challenge of delivering an experience.
