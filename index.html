Hey, I'm Spencer

Born in Salt Lake City to an orchestra of siblings - my first language was music and improvising ideas.

Up until 2013 I was performing and running a music studio full time. I loved it, but felt an incompleteness in my creativity as a result of neglecting visual design. I decided to apply to architecture schools and learn to code.

In 2014 I moved to North Carolina and picked up an internship at McKinney which led to a job for a small design/dev shop called 'The Experiment' (aptly named to mark the official beginning of my design career) which led to a job for a start-up out of SF called 'The Grid'.
